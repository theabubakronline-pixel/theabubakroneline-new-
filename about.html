My Story

The journey that shaped who I am today from where I started to where I'm heading.

01
2023

The Beginning

Starting the Digital Journey

In 2023, I completed my Matric in Computer Science and found myself at a crossroads. I needed to earn money to support my studies and lifestyle expenses, but there was no financial support from my family. This necessity became the driving force behind my digital journey.

I bought a laptop and arranged internet access from neighbors this was my first real investment in myself and my future. With limited resources but unlimited determination, I was ready to start learning and earning.

02
May 2023

Finding a Mentor

Meeting Anwar Roy & Starting with Guest Posting

I approached Anwar Roy, an expert in SEO and WordPress development who was already working and earning in the digital field. His company, MegaCodeLab, offers digital marketing services specializing in SEO and WordPress development. Anwar Roy's expertise in these areas and his successful business were a source of inspiration for me.

I went to him for consultation, asking about skills for freelancing that could help me earn quickly I needed a lot because I had no financial support. Anwar Roy advised me to start with guest posting services. He explained that this could help me earn early and learn valuable skills in digital marketing. This advice changed everything.

In May 2023, I started learning and offering guest posting services, helping people build backlinks and enhance their online presence. This was my first real step into the digital world. I posted many guest posts for clients, learning about SEO, content marketing, and building relationships with publishers. Each project taught me something new about how the digital marketing world works.

03
2023 - 2024

Building Skills

Guest Posting & Learning Digital Marketing & SEO

From 2023 to 2024, I was actively working on guest posting services and simultaneously learning digital marketing and SEO. This period was crucial for building my foundation in the digital marketing world. I focused on understanding how organic reach works, how to build quality backlinks, and how SEO strategies can help businesses grow without spending on ads.

I was particularly interested in free marketing strategies rather than paid advertising. This focus led me to dive deep into SEO, content marketing, organic social media growth, and email marketing. I wanted to master the art of growing businesses without relying on paid ads, which felt more sustainable and aligned with my goal of helping entrepreneurs who might not have large advertising budgets.

While I haven't explored paid advertising channels like Facebook Ads, YouTube Ads, TikTok Ads, Google Ads, Instagram Ads, or Pinterest Ads yet, I'm open to learning them in the future. I believe that understanding both free and paid marketing strategies will make me a more well-rounded marketer. My hope is to master paid advertising as well, combining it with free marketing tactics to create comprehensive growth strategies for businesses.

My journey from having no financial support to earning through freelancing taught me resilience, resourcefulness, and the importance of mentorship. I learned that with the right guidance and determination, you can build something from nothing.

04
January 2025

Professional Growth

Joining Ena Pragma Consulting

I joined Ena Pragma Consulting (Enapragma.co) as Assistant Marketing Manager in January 2025. Enapragma helps founders scale businesses using AI, Tech, and Marketing and this became a turning point in my career. Working there exposed me to how these three pillars could be combined to help businesses grow effectively.

The owner, CarlSapp, became not just a colleague but a friend and mentor who played a significant role in my professional development. Seeing how Enapragma helped founders scale businesses using AI, Tech, and Marketing inspired me to start learning how to build and scale businesses using the same approach. This experience gave me valuable hands-on experience in marketing strategies and business operations.

In May 2025, I transitioned to Software Engineer at the same company, expanding my technical skills while continuing to work with CarlSapp, a mentor I deeply respected. This dual role marketing and technology further reinforced my understanding of how AI, Tech, and Marketing work together to scale businesses. This experience taught me about resilience, adaptability, and the importance of maintaining professional relationships even through transitions.

05
February 2025 - Present

Building My Own Path

Founding The Abubakr Online & Siyah Noir Store

In February 2025, I founded The Abubakr Online as my personal brand and platform focused on helping entrepreneurs scale using AI, Technology, and Marketing. This became my primary focus where I share insights, build tools, and create content for the entrepreneurial community.

I also launched Siyah Noir Store (siyahnoir.store) as a hands-on learning project. This e-commerce venture serves as my real-world laboratory for testing AI implementation, marketing automation, and scaling strategies. Every lesson learned here directly informs how I help other entrepreneurs.

After leaving Ena Pragma Consulting in October 2025, I'm fully focused on building these ventures and learning through doing. The journey that started with a laptop, internet from neighbors, and a consultation with Anwar Roy has evolved into something much bigger. I'm now helping others start their own digital journeys, just like I did.
